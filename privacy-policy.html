Privacy Policy for FocusHacker

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

At FocusHacker (“we,” “our,” or “us”), we believe that your private productivity data should stay exactly that: private. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website (focushacker.app) and use the FocusHacker mobile application (the “App”).

1. Personal Data Collection — App Usage vs. Waitlist & Beta

App Usage: You do not need to create an account, register, or provide any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the App. We do not collect, harvest, or store any personal data from your app activity.

Waitlist & Beta Program: To join our waitlist or beta testing program, we collect limited personal information, including your email address and any other information you voluntarily provide during signup. This information is used solely to communicate with you about FocusHacker updates, beta access, and program participation. You may opt out of these communications at any time by contacting us at focushackerapp@gmail.com.

2. 100% Local Data Storage

All data you generate within the App—including your focus sessions, timers, tasks, history, and custom settings—is stored exclusively on your own device.

  • We do not operate external servers to collect this data.
  • We cannot access, view, modify, or delete your data because we never see it.
  • If you delete the App from your device, your local data will be permanently removed unless backed up via your personal iCloud settings.

3. Third-Party Services & Processing

While we do not collect your app usage data, we utilize trusted third-party platforms to distribute the App and handle basic operations:

Apple App Store: The App is distributed exclusively through the Apple App Store. Any purchases, subscriptions, or financial transactions are handled securely by Apple under their standard terms and privacy policy. We never receive or store your credit card or financial billing details.

Performance & Error Tracking: We may implement basic crash reporting and performance monitoring tools in the future to ensure the App runs smoothly. If implemented, these tools will only collect anonymous, aggregated technical data (such as device type, iOS version, and crash logs) that cannot be used to identify you personally.

Waitlist & Beta Management: We may use third-party email service providers to manage and communicate with waitlist and beta participants. These services handle your email address and communications in accordance with standard data protection practices.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website (focushacker.app) is a static promotional site. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other digital tracking technologies to monitor your browsing behavior across the web.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). Our App does not require registration or account creation, so children can use the core app features without providing personal data. For waitlist or beta signups, we request that participants be at least 13 years old.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted directly on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you can contact us at:

Email: focushackerapp@gmail.com
